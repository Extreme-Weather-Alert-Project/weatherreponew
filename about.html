Our Mission

WeatherGuard is dedicated to providing real-time extreme weather alerts to help communities stay safe and informed. With dynamic updates and detailed weather conditions, we aim to deliver accurate and timely information tailored to your location.

Our Platform

Our platform delivers current temperature, humidity, and wind speed, along with instant notifications of any active weather alerts such as thunderstorms, floods, or high winds. Designed with user experience in mind, WeatherGuard helps you plan and respond effectively to changing weather conditions.
